The formula for d is: $$d = \frac{M_1 - M_2}{\sqrt{(SD_1^2 + SD_2^2)  - (2*r*SD_1*SD_2)}} * \sqrt{2*(1-r)}$$
 
The t-test is not given in this example, as you do not have the information needed to calculate it.
